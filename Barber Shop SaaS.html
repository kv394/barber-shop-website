Custom Pages (Menus)

💡 Dynamic Smart Tags

Type these tags exactly as shown anywhere in your page content to automatically generate stunning, theme-aware widgets:

  • ${services} - Renders your service menu and "Book" buttons.
  • ${products} - Renders your sellable retail products.
  • ${team} - Renders a grid of your staff members with their bios.
  • ${gallery} - Displays a masonry gallery of your portfolio images.
  • ${reviews} - Shows all shop reviews and an interactive "Leave a Review" form.
  • ${contact} - Displays a beautiful map, hours, and contact details.

Booking Modal Embed Code

Embed the multi-step booking modal directly into your external website (WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, etc.). Follow these two simple steps:

Step 1: Add the Script Tag

Add this script tag to your <head> or right before the closing </body> tag of your website.

<script src="https://barbersaas.com/booking-modal.js" data-shop-id="cmn9kj24n0000lqzc7kcsmpst"></script>

Step 2: Add the Booking Button

Place this button anywhere on your page. The modal will open when the button is clicked.
Note: The button MUST have the id="barber-booking-btn" for it to work.

<button id="barber-booking-btn" style="padding: 10px 20px; background: #000; color: #fff; border: none; border-radius: 5px; cursor: pointer;">
  Book Appointment
</button>
