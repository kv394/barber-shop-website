Clubman Aftershave Lotion

$9.50

Clubman Aftershave Lotion is an iconic, widely-used men’s grooming product, famous for its essential role in the classic barbershop experience. For generations, it has served as the definitive finishing touch to a professional shave.

Scent Profile and Heritage
Often described as the quintessential "barbershop smell," it features a timeless, masculine fragrance. The scent profile is complex yet familiar, typically opening with:

  • Top Notes: Bright citrus (orange, bergamot, lemon) mixed with fresh green nuances.
  • Heart Notes: A floral heart of lavender, jasmine, and geranium.
  • Base Notes: A warm base of musk and oakmoss.

This distinct aroma evokes nostalgia for many, representing a long tradition of suave, impeccable grooming. The brand, Pinaud Clubman, has roots dating back to the early 19th century in France.

Uses and Benefits
Formulated to soothe post-shave skin, offering both comfort and hygiene. Its key benefits include:

  • Cooling and Exhilarating: Provides an immediate cooling sensation after a close shave.
  • Soothes Razor Burn: Helps to calm tenderness, minimize redness, and reduce post-shave irritation.
  • Aids in Healing: Contains antiseptic properties that help to close pores and assist in healing minor nicks.
  • Lingering Fragrance: Leaves a subtle, clean scent that acts as a light cologne.
